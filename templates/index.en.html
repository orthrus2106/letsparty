How does collaboration work?

A simple and clear process from inquiry to a memorable evening

  • Step 01

    You leave a request in any way convenient for you

  • Step 02

    We ask 3–5 clarifying questions to understand the scope and goals of your event.

  • Step 03

    We select the best game and interactive format specifically for your group.

  • Step 04

    We prepare a personalized scenario and align all technical details.

  • Step 05

    We run the game, engage the guests, and create that special festive atmosphere.

Why choose us?

We create experiences,
not just games

Producer mindset

We do not work from templates. Each format is tailored to the group, the mood, and the goal of the event. We ask guiding questions and value your context, so we can design unique details specifically for your company.

Lively and gentle engagement

We know how to build momentum without pressure. We never force anyone to participate. Active guests get room to play, and calmer guests feel comfortable and join naturally.

Sense of room and atmosphere

We carefully read the group’s mood and manage the evening’s pace so it never drops or becomes tiring. The game unfolds naturally, and the atmosphere stays warm and alive.

Multilingual format

We host events in Russian, Latvian, and English, including bilingual formats, so every guest feels included and stays part of the experience.

Flexibility and comfort

We adapt the format to people, not people to the format. We consider timing, venue, and guest mix to make the evening convenient and coherent for everyone.

Who we are

We don’t just run games — we create experiences

Let’s Party is a project created from friendship, energy, and love for vibrant celebrations.

We are Anastasia and Diana, two friends who have been at the center of events since childhood: drama club, school holidays, scripts, and parties for friends, simply because we truly loved it.

Back in eighth grade, after staging a play, we came up with the name Dynasty, combining our names and our dream of building something of our own. In 2025, that dream became a real event production company, Dynasty Events. We decided to start with the Let’s Party project.

Anastasia is a co-founder of the project and a host since 2022, with more than 500 events behind her. For her, it is not about just “running the format” but creating the exact atmosphere your group needs.

Diana is a co-founder with entrepreneurial experience since 2020. She easily connects with any person, can energize a room, align details, and create a comfortable, lively atmosphere.

We share the same vibe, energy, and attitude to work, and that is exactly what makes our team cohesive.
